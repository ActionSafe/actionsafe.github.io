About me

I am a graduate student in the School of Public Health at Peking University. I received my Bachelor degree (2023) in Medicine from Peking University. I also completed a minor program in Mathematics. My undergraduate research topic was mainly focused on machine learning and its applications in medicine. I am now working with Prof. Baosheng Liang on Biostatistics

Research interest

My Research area is Biostatistics and machine learning, with interests including recurrent event analysis, causal inference, Bayesian models, deep learning methods for survival analysis, and other applications in statistics. With the popularity of large language models, I also look forward to their applications in medicine and statistics.
