About me

I am a graduate student in the School of Public Health at Peking University. I received my Bachelor degree (2023) in Medicine from Peking University. I also complete a minor program in Mathematics. I am now working with Prof. Baosheng Liang on Biostatistics

Research interest

My Research area is Biostatistics and machine learning, with interests including graphical models, efficient Markov chain Monte Carlo methods and variational inference methods for Bayesian models, deep Bayesian learning/Bayesian deep learning, and various applications using probabilistic modeling.
